Welcome! This app/website will help you figure out how strong your password is. You will be able to see the strength on a meter that gives you a mark out of 100. It will give you notifications and alerts so you can improve your password. If you are having trouble improving your password, you can also go to our "Tips" tab. It will give you useful information on how to create a strong password. Be safe!

About the Creator:

Password Strength Meter is an app used to measure the security of passwords of users of this app. This app was created by Yichen Xiao and Daniel David Lee for an experiment for their science class. The experiment was to create an app to detect the security of passwords and try different passwords randomly generated and given by people to see how to create the strongest password possible.
